Objective

The objective of creating a website for a Jam sessions event is to provide workers with a fun and relaxing way to spend their evenings during the week. The website will serve as a hub for information on the event, providing up-to-date details on the date, time, and location of the jam sessions. Additionally, the website will offer a glimpse into the "before," "during," and "after" of the event, giving visitors an idea of what they can expect if they attend. By creating a website for this event, we hope to attract a wider audience and make it easier for people to find information about the jam sessions. The website will be designed with a modern and user-friendly interface, making it easy for visitors to navigate and find the information they need.

Research

After creating a series of surveys, interviews and field research, were an important factor. Most people are available to attend events during the weekdays, and would like to see events held after work hours. Participants emphasized the importance of an engaging and dynamic atmosphere at the event, as well as good quality sound and music.

Attendees also highlighted the importance of clear communication and up-to-date information regarding the event schedule and location. Using this research, we have created Gotblues, a website for a Jam sessions event that aims to address the concerns and preferences of our audience.

The website will provide up-to-date information on the event, including the date, time, location, and lineup of musicians. Additionally, the website will feature an engaging and user-friendly interface, allowing visitors to easily navigate the site and find the information they need.
