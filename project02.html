Research

We conducted extensive research by interviewing and surveying people of different ages, including 40% men. Our findings showed that a vast majority, 90%, take care of their skin on a daily or regular basis, and 83.9% are most worried about dark circles and expression lines. This emphasizes the need for an effective solution to help people maintain healthy skin. Moreover, we discovered that 56% of our respondents actively use skincare and search for products that best suit their skin.

However, despite this interest, 90% of them do not have the time to dedicate to a routine. Interestingly, 46.9% of our respondents were aware of facial yoga's existence, but never practiced it due to lack of information and adaptability. This highlights the importance of creating an app that is easy to use and accessible to everyone. Finally, our research showed that 75% of respondents do not use similar apps because they find them too complicated,

struggle with language barriers or have difficulty making payments. Our findings are invaluable in ensuring that Yofi addresses these concerns and offers a user-friendly and accessible solution to improve the health and appearance of their skin.
